Let's get in About Us.

SYNERGY, is a leading Omani information technology, consulting and business process services company specializing in state-of-the-art software & technology solutions and services to all organizations in Oman. With over 50% Omanization, SYNERGY strives to achieve the nationalistic vision of sustainable economic growth through skilled and competent Omani resources. SYNERGY achieved tremendous success in a short span of time and is continuously growing and expanding its scope of services delivering excellence to its customers across the country.

SYNERGY is the leader in providing customers with valuable, convenient, relevant, and enjoyable online experience through a diversity of solutions and platforms.

Synergy has been working with governments and enterprises ona range of business verticals across the globe delivering innovative enterprise intelligence solutions and platforms for seamless collaboration within and also for achieving customer experience.

A comprehensive set of platform-based solutions, coupled with a wide range of IT services has uniquely positioned the company to address the dynamic requirements of a variety of customers.

Our delivery model provides the best services and resources drawn from a committed pool of technologists and business analysts to provide optimal solutions. Synergy integrates its solution platforms and services to allow the customers to undertake technology-based business transformation that allows reorganization in line with today’s dynamic digital business environment.

Our Principles:

Right Information within the Right Context to the Right Person at the Right Time for the Right Business Purpose.

Our Quality Policy:

We strive to provide solutions and service with emphasis on continuous improvement, pro-active approach, build long-term client relationships based on mutual trust and respect. Everything we do reflects the mission and the principles that make it possible.

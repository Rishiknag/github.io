About us
A congregation of industry experts who have been triumphant and extremely prudent as SMEs in Accounting, Audit, Assurance, Advisory and Technology domain. The core team has been at the forefront of conceptualizing niche delivery in core Finance & Accounting domain.
Catering to enterprises across various domains like Education, Pharma, IT, Infrastructure, Manufacturing and many more, the service delivery has keen focus on building robust processes, business continuity, risk assessment, advisory and providing niche consulting services. With the passion of building service delivery solutions for enterprises of all scales and sizes, the members eventually came together as a composite firm – 4AT. Our management team brings the prolific ‘Big 4’ expertise leveraging niche competencies in accounting landscape.
4AT signifies highest standards of professional service delivery encompassing offshoring opportunities in Accounting, Audit, Assurance, Advisory and Technology categories.
Our service-line not only focuses on building accountability, controllership and value, but also drives cost effective framework to manage critical functions. Our solutions take a detour from ‘One size fit all’ philosophy to provide customized solutions for specific needs.
We are engaged in service delivery mainly in the areas of Consulting, Advisory and Audit for premium large corporations. Besides that, we have strong competencies in Technical Accounting given our long-term service for large enterprises. With a firm focus on bringing cost benefits for our clients, we have built a robust framework based on ‘Build Operate Transfer’ model that provides significant benefits and value for our clients.
Who we are?
Domain experts with expansive industry experience across varied sectors providing niche services in the areas of Accounting, Audit & Assurance, Advisory and Technology.

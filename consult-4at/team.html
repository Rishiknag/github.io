Aruna Sharma

MANAGING PARTNER

Aruna has over 15 years of experience in public accounting and financial services. A qualified Chartered Accountant, and an MBA in Finance and Marketing, Aruna is an astute professional with key domain experience in providing the assurance services for SEC registered companies.

She was instrumental as a part of the pioneer group to set-up offshore services for one of the Big 4 firms in India. She has served companies in varied industries such as Power and Utilities, Consumer products and Investment management. She has a rich experience in Benefit plan audit and M&A transaction services.

Chetana RG Kaasam
